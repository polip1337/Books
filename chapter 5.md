Zack and Aiko sat on opposite ends of the small table. An open screen between them displaying his stats. The potion Aiko gave him and the Regenerating status, fixed him back to full health in about half an hour. Bit slow to use in the middle of a battle but good for recuperating after one. He looked at his hands and the faint lines of scars still visible. Aiko told him they should fade in a couple of hours.

The woman fidgeted slightly, her cheeks flushed, her gaze drifting anywhere but at Zack. Her fingers played with a loose thread on her sleeve. After the battle Zack offered his place to clean their wounds, rest and discuss some stuff.

“First things first, you should have 20 free stats. You get 5 per level and you get to assign them as you see fit. You didn't do it yet?” Zack shook his head. “ Good, don't do that yet, we should talk about what each one does so you can make a good decision”

Zack nodded again, it made perfect sense to him.

"Okay, Zack," she said, clearing her throat and focusing on the screen. "Let's go over your stats. Your Vitality is 16, which is quite good. 10 is the human baseline. Most people have their ‘natural’ stats in the range of 5 to 15. Where 15 is the top human condition.”

“How come my Vitality is 16 then?” Zack asked in confusion.

“You get immediately boosted when you hit level 1. It's impossible to say what your stats were before the awakening”

“And you had me thinking I was a genius with my 15 Intelligence” Zack laughed.

“You might as well have been we don't know what it was pre-awakening” Aiko responded seriously. “But we will get to that. Anyway, vitality—this determines your overall health 10 points for the first 10 levels in Vitality then 5 extra for each level.”

"But my health is 174 out of 174.” Zack nodded, glancing at the numbers. He thought for a while. “Does my Strength and Endurance provide a bonus to that? Hmm since they are both 11 and I have additional 44 health above what you describe, it could be any distribution of 4 points per level between each of them”

Aiko’s brows went up in surprise and maybe a bit of admiration. ”That's a good deduction. The distribution is actually 2 extra health for both Strength and Endurance.”

“So how does health work?” Zack rubbed his chin. “It might be good for games but i’d guess it's hard to reduce someone's health to a single number.”

“Correct again.” Aiko confirmed “The long and short of it is that it sometimes seems rather arbitrary, or we just dont know everything. The general rule of thumb is that short term wounds decrease your current health and not the maximum. Sickness either decrease your maximum or you get a status effect”

“Like i got ‘Deafened’, with a capital D, by that Voidstalkers attack” Zack interrupted.

“Exactly, so cancer would decrease your maximum and you would succumb to wounds in combat quicker, which at least makes some sort of sense” Aiko sighed. “Where it really gets weird is if you were to suffer a permanent wound it would decrease your maximum too. Using common sense a loss of mobility in the elbow should not affect your ability to survive, let's say a claw wound. But it clearly does.”

“Noone tried to figure this out?” This time it was Zack’s eyebrows going up.

“Oh they did try, it just never amounted to anything useful so everyone collectively shrugged and left it as is. I mean we see the rewriting of reality on the regular so …” Aiko explained with a shrug of her own.

Zack was unsatisfied but decided he wouldn't learn more about that at that moment. His eyes  settled back on the screen. "Next is Endurance”

“That ones simple. It affects your stamina and how long you can sustain physical activities. Using skills uses either stamina or mana so be careful with that."

"Got it," Zack said, leaning back slightly.. "Strength, Dexterity, and Intelligence are all at 11, 11, and 15 respectively. What do those mean for me?"

"Strength and Dexterity are also quite straightforward," Aiko responded, her fingers tapping lightly on her knee. "Strength influences your physical power, like how hard you can hit or how much you can lift. Dexterity affects your agility and precision and maybe the most importantly pure speed. As in both reaction time and the pace at which you move. If you saw a high level battle you probably wouldn’t be even able to follow it due to Dexterity difference”

“That one sounds important then” Zack said, deep in thought planning his build.

“It's a core requirement for most high level combatants. Above a certain level, when you really go into the superhuman, it doesn't matter how hard you hit or how powerful your magic is if you can't react fast enough. There are some mages that work around this with area of effect spells but that has downsides too” Aiko’s gaze was unfocused like she was remembering a battle she saw in the past.

“I have my Scorching Aura with an AoE but and my other skills seem to be defense and improving of my body” Zack thought out loud

“If you want my advice” Aiko looked at him questioningly and Zack nodded “You should be getting another active skill before you hit level 10, it should give you more hints about the direction you should take your stats. Having said that, you will probably need at least some points in most of them later anyway. Why it matters right now is that good or bad distribution can make your fights easier, or harder, while you get there.

"Intelligence is 15, which is quite high," she continued, the corner of her mouth twitching upward. "This helps with your mana pool and the effectiveness of your spells. Your Mana is at 150 so as you probably guessed 10 points for each one in Intelligence. That's what's immediately important. Especially in the earlier levels it will be a bottleneck for how many abilities you can use in combat.”

“I experienced that in our last battle” Zack said thinking back to when he had to disable his Scorcing Aura during the fight with the bull. Now that he considered it this particular skill grew only by 2 levels compared to most others growing by 3, it was probably because he didn’t use it that much. It just made sense.

Aiko continued, full teacher mode engaged. “Also increasing this stat won't make you any smarter per say. You won't be able to get a higher IQ score or make connections you wouldn't be able otherwise. What it will do for you however is increase both your capacity for storing information and your recollection. Get it high enough and you will basically have a functionally eidetic memory."

"Damn, no solving world hunger with the power of my brain then." Zack replied, his eyes meeting hers for a moment. She quickly looked away, her cheeks reddening again. "And Wisdom? It's at 12."

"Wisdom is even more nebulous," Aiko explained, her voice a bit softer. "What everybody agrees is that it affects mana regeneration, so it gives you more staying power, or lets you get ready for the next fight faster. There is a lot of discussion whether it affects your decision making at all. People, at least most of them, get wiser with age and higher level people are older."

“So, hard to tell?” Zack summarized.

“Pretty much just that,” Aiko said with an apologetic smile.

Zack smiled, trying to put her at ease. "So, I'm basically a well-rounded guy with a knack for magic and a decent head on my shoulders."

Aiko laughed lightly, the tension in the room easing a bit. "Yes, you could say that. You got a good base. But remember, these stats are just numbers. How you use them in real situations is what really counts."

Zack nodded thoughtfully. "Thanks, Aiko. I appreciate you going over this with me."

Aiko stood up, smoothing out her clothes. "No problem, Zack. I said I would help you and this is the least i could do”

As she headed for the door, Zack caught himself smiling. They'd stumbled through the awkwardness, but something about her straightforwardness put him at ease. He trusted her—not just with information, but to watch his back.

“Hey Aiko, we should swap numbers so we can contact each other” Zack pulled out his phone.

Aiko almost slapped her forehead with her palm ”Oh my god, obviously we should, sorry”.

“No need to be sorry, there has been a lot going on lately” Zack calmed her down.

They exchanged their contact information. “We both could use some rest, ill call you in a couple of days for further training, i have some stuff i need to take care of too” Aiko said mysteriously. Zack didn't want to pry so he just nodded. After that they said their goodbyes and Zack closed the door behind the silver-haired woman.

“Time to distribute me some stats” he said to himself.

Zack sat down on the couch, staring intently at the screen displaying his stats. He had twenty points to distribute and needed to make strategic decisions. First, he allocated five points to Vitality, increasing it from 16 to 21. He reasoned that in his line of work, being able to withstand more damage was crucial.

His recent encounters with the Voidstalkers had shown him the importance of resilience, he shuddered at the memory of how his hands looked after the battle. He was really grateful for Aiko’s regeneration potion.

Next, Zack considered his Endurance and Dexterity. He added four points to Endurance, raising it from 11 to 15. The lack of stamina was his biggest issue in the latest fight and he felt like he needed a lot of it. Compounding the issue was the fact that if he would get an active skill soon he would burn through it even faster. He added an additional point, bringing it to a total of 16.

He also decided to allocate five points to Dexterity, bringing it up to 16. This increase would have been a godsend in his last battle, even if he was 5% faster it would make dodging that much easier. Following Aiko’s advice that being able to move quickly could sometimes be more important than sheer strength.

Finally, Zack turned his attention to Intelligence and Wisdom both would increase the staying power he had. His skills mostly passively consumed mana so he was on a timer as soon as a fight started. Intelligence would increase the starting point of that timer while wisdom would make it count down slower. Ideally he wanted to offset the passive drain of Scorching Aura and Armor of Flame with Wisdom so he could use them indefinitely.

That's why he used the three points on Wisdom, increasing it from 12 to 15. Then he added the last two points to Intelligence, moving it from 15 to 17. The higher Intelligence would not only increase his mana pool but also enhance the potency of his spells.

The last one was a tough decision as both his active skills scaled with INT, but he just wanted the mana regeneration more.

There was one last thing before ending the day. Zack went around the room and closed all the blinders on the windows. Then he stood in the middle of his cramped apartment and hoped he wouldn’t set anything on fire. He was curious how his new stats and levels would affect his skills.

As he activated his Armor of Flame, the familiar sensation of heat enveloped his hands. This time, however, the flames covered more than his hands, they extended up to his forearms. The flames were denser, more solid than before. They flickered and roared, the heat emanating from them greater than before.

They even had a solid heft to them as if he was wearing actual armor. That was on fire. And shimmered with hues of orange and blue. He smiled at the sheer absurdity of it all.

Zack looked around. The apartment was bathed in the light of his flame armor, casting dancing shadows on the walls. Zack marveled at the sight, moving his arms and watching the shadows move with him. The heat radiating from the armor was fierce, and he immediately turned the skill off when one of the pillows started smoking.

Following that little misadventure he decided to wait with activating Scorching Aura until he was in a more flame resistant setting. After all, it had a warning in the description that it could set stuff on fire.
